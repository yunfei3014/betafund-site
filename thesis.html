Our Thesis

We believe the next generation of companies won't just use AI — they'll be built for AI.

Today's software is designed for humans. Tomorrow's software will be designed for autonomous systems. We invest behind three long-term shifts shaping the next decade.

01

Token-native

Value creation, ownership, incentives, and coordination become programmable. Tokens aren't an add-on — they're the operating system for digital economies. We believe every internet-native network will eventually have its own economic layer.

02

Agent-native

Software will increasingly communicate with software. Products won't just be used by humans — they'll be read by agents, written by agents, and operated by agents. The next billion users may not be people.

03

Self-improving systems

The most valuable companies won't simply automate work. They'll continuously learn, rewrite themselves, improve their own workflows, and compound intelligence over time. Every execution loop becomes another training loop.

may not be people.
